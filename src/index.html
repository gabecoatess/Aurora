SUPPORT BETA

Problem

Since the COVID-19 pandemic, students, teachers, and parents have a tough time keeping track of student grades, attendance, and overall academic responsibilities. This is because it is all spread out across multiple different platforms, which breaks the unification and simplicity of having one singular school platform.

Solution

In order to solve this problem, our team has dedicated time and effort into creating an application that can be used across a wide range of devices to display useful student information to save everyone some time. All unified and native. You can view the features list on our GitHub.

How

There are great challanges that we are willing to face head on in order to provide a positive user experience for all of our members. We are students that have decided to come together to create a web application, native desktop application, and mobile application of our vision. Using a multitude of tools available to us, we will spend every bit of time we have to developing this application. You can follow up on our GitHub.
